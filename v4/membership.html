Exclusive Benefits and Privileges:

New Hong Kong VIP Card members get to enjoy:

  • 10% discount on selected food items.
  • 5% discount on seasonal / festive products.

Specially for customers who celebrate their birthdays at New Hong Kong Restaurant:

  • You can apply for a New Hong Kong VIP Card absolutely FREE.
  • Upon sign up, you can use your VIP card IMMEDIATELY to obtain a special 15% discount on that day.

Application & Renewal:
New application for membership is RM20 for the first year, while renewal is only RM10 annually.
